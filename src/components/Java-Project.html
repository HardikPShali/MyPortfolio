Java Project

Project Overview

This Project is a conversion of DotNet-Project (InsuranceHub) into Java.

Project Details

Admin Panel can Manage Companies who are giving insurance to customers and also associated with InsuranceHub
It can also manage various types of Insurances(Insert, Update or delete)
It can also manage Hospitals that are giving insurance to customers and also associated with InsuranceHub
It can also manage Insurance Policies
feedbacks from Various customers are listed in this panel
Various inquiries which are made by various customers also listed in this panel
Admin can also manage customers of term insurance and health insurance. admin can change the status of payment of various customers.
various customers can sign in and login in client panel
In Client Panel, customers can see different policies of term and health insurance and then they can select one or more policies as per their requirements but they can choose or select only one policy at a time and they need to provide the details which are needed to be given and after that, they can pay for that policy.
Invoice and Purchase History features are also available in this client panel.
customer can also update their profile.

  • Technologies Used: JakartaEE, JSF, HTML, CSS, Javascript, SQL
